Do more with a Bankly POS

Bankly launched in 2018 with a vision to enable financial inclusion
and build the digital guardrails required to provide affordable financial
products and services to the informal sector using a (hybrid) physical
and digital model.
In our first two years of full operation, we have served over 2 million
Nigerians, and empowered over 30,000 entrepreneurs within the
informal financial sector.
Our commitment is to continue to help more underbanked and
underserved Nigerians achieve full, financial inclusion and affordable
and final services.
